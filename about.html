About Us

Our History

Steel and Fabrication Solutions is a fast-growing company established in the year 2020 to provide specialist services in steel fabrication and various steel modifications for both domestic and commercial areas. Through careful selection and Research & Development, Steel and Fabrication Solutions is a multi-disciplinary with a broadened business base. With a combination of the professional know-how, experience and proven technology with an approach tailored to your specific needs, we help you to get any product you require. Our staff includes qualified mechanical and electrical designers and engineers, coupled with business administrators and technicians associated with years of experience to bring the best solution to any challenge.

  • Pre cast moulds for the concrete industries.
  • Hoopers and shoots
  • Staircases for industrial and residential
  • Balastrading for industrial and residential
  • Burglar bars
  • Sliding gates
  • T -top and rails for the marine industry
  • Fire places, custom made to clients design
  • Domes for glamping camping, either Pyramid or round, they come in many shapes, we will fabricate to your needs.
  • Trailers
  • Maintenance

Company Vision

To be the leaders in South Africa and throughout the African continent in providing unrivalled steel modifications and fabrication solutions built to last and by providing our clients with unmatched performance and the highest-level of satisfaction in the industry.

Company Mission

To deliver products of value, highest quality and long-lasting solutions to our Clients through cost effective designs, engineering and manufacturing services in the industry.
